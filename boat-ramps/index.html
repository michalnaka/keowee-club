Quick answers

How many public boat ramps are on Lake Keowee?

Five public launch points: South Cove County Park, High Falls County Park, Mile Creek County Park, Keowee Town Landing, and Stamp Creek Landing. The three county parks add camping, docks, or swim areas; the two landings are simple ramps.

How do I get a boat onto Lake Jocassee?

Devils Fork State Park in Salem is the only public boat access on Jocassee. On summer weekends it's busy — arrive early.

Does the lake level affect launching?

Yes — when Keowee runs a few feet below full pond, the shallow ends of some ramps get skinny. The live chips at the top of this page show today's level; South Cove sits near the deepest water on the lake and is the usual low-water choice.
